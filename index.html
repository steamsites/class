STEAM Education

STEAM is a new and exciting movement in education that meets the needs of a 21st century economy. The acronym stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and math and encomposes most of the key components that many employers, educators, and parents feel as important for students to thrive in the present and rapidly approaching future.

HTML Websites

There are many benefits of learning to code -critical thinking skills, problem-solving skills, becoming more fluent with technology, and possible career paths. The grade 7/8 students learned basic HTML and CSS, two coding languages used to build websites, as one of our projects in STEAM. Many students went beyond what was introduced to them in our STEAM classes and got pretty creative with the outlook of their websites.

The student websites are linked below so please make sure to check them out. If your interested in learning to make a website from scratch using HTML, you should check out the HTML Tutorial video posted below the student website links.

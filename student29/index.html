My favourite sport: Volleyball

As you can see from the title, my favourite sport is volleyball. I love volleyball. Eventhough I'm not on a rep volleyball team, but I'm on a team for my school. I play well during school. I play the position setter, the person that passes the ball to the hitter. The hitter spikes the ball at the other team. Setting is a very stressful position. You need to rely on your passers to pass you a good ball to be able to set the hitter. If they don't give you a good ball, its hard to set the hitter. I love volleyball just because its so satisfying when you set the hitter and they hit in the other teams face. Thats why volleyball is my favourite sport.

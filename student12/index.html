Rhythm and blues is a music genre combing blues, gospel, and jazz. It was first performed by African-American artists. Nowadays, Contemperary R&B is a popular music style combining soul, funk, pop, and hip hop music. It began during the disco era in the 1970's. To add on, Contemperary R&B originated in Housten, Atlanta, Toronto, Montreal and other cities. Popular comtemporary R&B songs are No Scrubs by TLC, No Diggity by Blackstreet, and Boo'd Up by Ella Mai.

The Best Cookie Recipe of all Time

By:Maiura

Ingredients:

-2 cups all purpose flour

-2 tsp baking soda

-1 1/2 tsp salt

-1 1/2 sticks of butter

-3/4 cup golden brown sugar

-1/4 cup granulated white sugar

-1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

-1 egg

-1 cup chococlate chips

-1 cup salted caramel chocolate chips

Directions:

1. Line a baking tray with parchment paper or grease the tray with butter and preheat the oven to 325 degrees F or 165 degrees C.

2. In a medium bowl, mix the melted butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until well combined. Then beat in the egg and vanilla until completely smooth and creamy.

3. In a seperate bowl, sift together the flour, baking soda, and salt.

4. Slowly mix in little bits of the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients until both of them are well combined.

5. Then pour in the chocolate chips and mix them together until they are completely mixed in with one another

6. Take out approximately a tablespoon of the cookie dough and roll it into a ball, then place in on your preppared baking tray. Repeat this proccess until you run out of cookie dough or until you make the amount of cookies that is to your liking, the cookies should be 2 or 3 inches apart from one another.

7. Place the tray in the oven and bake it for 10-15 minutes, or until they are golden brown. lastly, take the cookies out of the oven and wait for them to cool down and enjoy!

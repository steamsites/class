All About Border Collies

Roses are grey

Violets are grey I'm a dog, everything's grey

Border Collies are not a hypoallergenic dog breed. Their life span is 10-17 years. A male is 14-20 kg. A female is 12-19 kg. They are tenacious, responsive, energetic, keen, alert and intelligent. Border collies are in different colours. They are in black, white, blue, brindle, red merle, lilac, chocolate, sable, liver, blue merle, sable merle, red, and gold. Border collie males' height are 48-56cm. Border collie females' height are 46-53cm. They are also known as herding dogs.

All About Golden Retrievers

The Golden Retriever is a large-sized breed of dog bred as gun dogs to retrieve shot waterfowl, such as ducks and upland game birds, during hunting and shooting parties, and were named 'retriever' because of their ability to retrieve shot game undamaged. Their life span is 10-12 years. A female weighs 25-32 kg. A male weighs 30-34 kg. They are kind, trustworthy, intelligent, reliable, confident, and friendly. Golden retrievers come in different colours. They come in Cream, Dark Golden, Light Golden and Golden. The height for a female golden retriever 51-56 cm. The height for a male golden retriever is 56-61 cm.
